# policies/data-privacy-policy.md

## Data Privacy & Retention Policy

- No PII or sensitive user data is stored unless required and documented.
- All memory, logs, and archives are pruned and rotated per retention policy.
- Data retention defaults: 30 days for logs, 90 days for memory, 1 year for archives.
- All data access is logged and auditable.
- Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant regulations is required.
- Automated checks for PII and compliance are planned.

---

> Update this policy as new data types or regulations are introduced.
